    Who are we?

    VijijiGreen is a grass-root initiative whose goal is to promote sustainable practices in rural farming villages in Kenya. We take a holistic approach to sustainability by looking at the three major pillars: economic, social and environmental.

    Our pilot project is based in Meru Kenya where we are working with subsistent farmers to improve their farming practices. Our goal is to promote agroforestry, afforestation and reforestation especially in the places that are now bare. We not only aim to tackle climate change but also to improve the livelihoods of the communities through improved ecosystems.
